Edmonton Grown

Born and raised in Edmonton Alberta, Reid fits the mold for what the city is most known for, being kindhearted and inviting, while never getting used to the winter.

Let's do Business

In his 'finding himself phase', Reid attended Macewan University and graduated with a Bachelor Of Commerce after draggin out his degree for 5.5 years. He doesn't recommend going into management, because it's soul draining and absolute hell for anyone creative.

Writing A New Chapter

Reid had a passion and talent writing stories, building technical projects, and working with other creatives. He started reading screenscripting and coding books, wrting scripts and doing various projects, like coding a magic mirror, never really sure if this would stay a hobby or if it would lead somewhere. In 2022, Reid was accepting into the VFS Writing program. Although he was positive he'd leave as a TV writing, Reid found his calling was narrative design for video games. He with left VFS with Lord Baser cup, a fire and talent for video game narrative and life long talented friends who he still works with on a weekly basis.

Reprogramming Life

Upon graduating, Reid continued to write scripts and turn his designs into playable games. He started by participates in game jams, however, either none of his teams wanted to create narrative focused games, the scripts he wrote never made it into the final version, or the programmers didn't know how to build a narrative system. With the basic knowledge Reid had from a couple coding projects, he decided to learn how to make the games he wanted to make. After a year of work, he made a massive Twine game with a dice mechanic in a visual novel system, a FPS that had a unique lightball mechanic, and a visual novel using Inkle in unity. However, he had reached the end of what the internet could teach him, so he applied for the Future schoolarship at VFS. Two months later, he got a phone call informing him he won, which he remembers as the best day of his life.
